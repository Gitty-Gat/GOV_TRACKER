Score guide

What the scorecards mean

Truth verdict

A quick signal for whether visible work lines up with stated priorities.

Formula: 60% promise coverage + 40% delivery quality.

Efficiency metric

Shows how much bill work the office moves each year.

Formula: (sponsored weight + cosponsored weight + advancement bonus) / years in office.

Delivery index

Shows how far matched bills actually moved.

Formula: average weighted score across matched priorities.
